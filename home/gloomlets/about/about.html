About Domak

This story was first self-published as a fantasy-adventure novel under the name "El grupo Domak" in 2015. The story you're gonna find here is very similar to that one, but not the same. If you have read "El grupo Domak" you might want to think of this one as a Domak from a parallel universe.

I started writing Domak when I was a kid (these characters are basically my childhood OCs), the story has had many correction instances and revissions and has evolved into something I'm proud of and I want to share with the world, I hope you enojoy it.

About the Author

Hi! I'm Lumaga, the creator of this comic. I live in Uruguay. Anyways, I'm out here making the stories I'd like to see more of in the world.

About Four Leaf

Four Leaf is a webcomic that I started publishing in 2016, and later restarted in 2018 when it became a webtoon original. It reached its conclusion after five years of publication.That one is also a fantasy story, but very different from Domak. Acerca de Domak

Esta historia fue auto-publicada por primera vez como una novela de aventura y fantasía bajo el nombre "El grupo Domak" en 2015. La historia que encontrarán aquí es muy similar a aquella, pero no la misma. Si has leído "El grupo Domak" quizás quieras pensar en esta como un Domak de un universo paralelo.

Empecé a escribir Domak cuando era pequeña (estos personajes son básicamente los OCs de mi infancia), la historia ha tenido muchas instancias de corrección y revisiones y ha evolucionado en algo de lo que me enorgullezco y que quiero compartir con el mundo, espero que la disfruten.
